# Employee Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain professionalism, integrity, and respect in the workplace. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior will not be tolerated.

# Work Hours and Attendance
Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees are required to clock in and out using the companyâ€™s attendance system. Any absence must be reported in advance to the reporting manager.

# Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 12 days of annual leave, 14 days of sick leave, and additional leaves for maternity, paternity, and special circumstances. Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days in advance, except in emergency cases.

# Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to two days per week, subject to managerial approval. Remote workers are expected to be available online during work hours and maintain productivity levels.

# Performance Evaluation
Employee performance will be reviewed biannually. Key evaluation criteria include work quality, adherence to deadlines, teamwork, and innovation. High performers will be eligible for bonuses and promotions.

# Travel and Expense Reimbursement
Business travel expenses, including airfare, accommodation, and meals, will be reimbursed as per company policy. Employees must submit valid receipts within 15 days of travel completion.

# IT Security and Data Privacy
Employees must follow IT security protocols, including using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and avoiding unauthorized software installations. Data privacy guidelines must be strictly adhered to when handling sensitive company and customer data.

# Workplace Harassment Policy
The company maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards harassment of any kind. Employees experiencing or witnessing harassment should report incidents immediately to HR.

# Exit and Termination Policy
Employees resigning from their positions must serve a 30-day notice period. Final settlements, including unpaid salaries and benefits, will be processed within 45 days of the last working day.

# Employee Benefits
Employees are entitled to health insurance, wellness programs, and learning and development allowances. Details on benefits can be accessed through the HR portal.

# Code of Ethics
Employees must adhere to the highest ethical standards. Bribery, conflicts of interest, and fraudulent activities are strictly prohibited. Violations may result in disciplinary actions, including termination.
